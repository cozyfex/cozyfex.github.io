## Setting GOPATH

The `GOPATH` is where external packages installed with `go get` are stored.

{{< admonition type=tip title="GOPATH" open=true >}}
In `GoLand`, you can set `GOAPTH` for each modules in `Settings > Go > GOPATH`.
{{< /admonition >}}
