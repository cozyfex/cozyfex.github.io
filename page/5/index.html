Kubernetes Services

CozyFex published on 2021-05-09 included in kubernetes k8s orchestration
What is the Services in the Kubernetes? The Services are ways to communicate users to frontend, frontend to backend, and backend to extra datasource. The Services enable loose coupling in between microservices in application. The Services are in the labels/selector matched Nodes and so you can access to the Nodes with configured Port. The Service that is in the Node can access labels/selector matched PODs with the configured Port in the Node.
